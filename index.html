About Jets Elementary

Discover our rich history, mission, and vision for educating the next generation.

Our Story

Jets Elementary School was founded in August 2023 by its visionary founder, Madam Dianah Omariba, with a clear Mission: to provide a nuturing, learning centered environment where every child has the opportunity to grow,discorver, and succeed.

Jets opened its doors with an initial enrollment of 50 learners. Despite being a young institution, the school quickly demonstrated its commitment to excellence. Within just two months of operation, jets proudly celebrated its first graduating learner, making a significance milestone in its early journey.

Today, jets Elementary stands as a growing internationan-minded institution, committed to excellence, inclusivity, and preparing young learners to thrive in global community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, student-centered education that empowers children to become confident, compassionate, and responsible global citizens. We strive to create a learning environment that celebrates diversity, encourages critical thinking, and fosters a lifelong love of learning.

Our Vision

We envision a school where every child discovers their unique talents, develops strong character, and acquires the skills needed to thrive in an ever-changing world. Our vision is to be a leading elementary school known for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and a strong sense of community.

School Aims

  • To provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment
  • To foster academic excellence through innovative teaching methods
  • To develop students' social, emotional, and physical well-being
  • To promote respect, responsibility, and good citizenship
  • To encourage creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills
  • To build strong partnerships with parents and the wider community

Food Allergies & Special Diets

We understand that some students have food allergies or special dietary needs. Our kitchen staff is trained to handle these situations with care and attention.

If your child has food allergies or requires a special diet:

  • Please inform the school administration during registration
  • Provide medical documentation if necessary
  • Meet with our kitchen manager to discuss specific needs
  • We will create a customized meal plan for your child
  • Separate preparation areas and utensils are used for allergy-safe meals

Common allergies we accommodate include: nuts, dairy, gluten, eggs, and seafood. We maintain strict protocols to prevent cross-contamination.

For students with severe allergies, we can arrange for parents to provide packed meals if preferred. Our goal is to ensure every child enjoys safe, nutritious meals while at school.
